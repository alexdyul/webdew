About research

data

Scans of the protocols of the Academy of Sciences for 1725-1743 are available to us. Site of the Russian Academy of Sciences. Publication of 1897 of average quality. Protocol languages: • Latin from November 13, 1725 to November 08, 1733; • German from November 11, 1734 to December 31, 1741; • Latin from January 9, 1742 to December 23, 1743

results

In 60.42% of cases, academics came from German lands. The average age for admission to the academy was 30.66 years. Equal distribution of the number of academicians by scientific classes. 16 people, or 33% of the total for mathematical, physical and human sciences. The most popular specialties of academicians are mathematics and history, 18.75% of the total number of specialties. There is a slight trend: the more adult person becomes an academician, the higher his performance in the activities of the academy.
